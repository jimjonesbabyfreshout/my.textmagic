What is SMS marketing?

SMS or text marketing means sending promotional campaigns, transactional alerts, or time-sensitive offers for marketing purposes via text messaging. SMS marketing can yield incredible results for small- and medium-sized businesses because of its low costs, excellent delivery, and open rates.

Text messages represent the most direct line of communication with prospects. As long as there is consent for contacting, SMS will remain the most effective marketing strategy for most businesses.

How can I get started with text marketing for my business?

Text marketing is widely used by local businesses such as dental clinics, real-estate agencies, restaurants, and more. Online businesses and eCommerce marketers are also beginning to adopt it as a strategy to connect with their clients.

Before you get started, you need to understand that SMS marketing is permission-based. You can only send a text to a number if that person wants to hear from you this way. Never send unsolicited texts to your customer base.

Here’s how you can get started with SMS marketing:

  • Step #1: Create an account with the TextMagic bulk text messaging software.
  • Step #2: Reserve a dedicated number for your business.
  • Step #3: Import your existing contact list with a few clicks and send an opt-in text to receive express written consent for your future communications.
  • Step #4: Start sending your first text marketing campaign. With TextMagic, you can compose personalized text campaigns that you can forward to your entire customer base or specific audience segments. Texts can also be scheduled months in advance, sent directly from your app via API, distributed with email to SMS, and more.
  • Step #5: Create a branded sign-up form to start collecting phone numbers from your website.
  • Step #6: Automate frequent interactions. TextMagic allows you to configure automations based on various triggers, behaviors, or filters. Unlike manually composing an SMS marketing campaign, automated texts trigger every time your customer base takes a specific action.
  • Step #7: Experiment and measure. TextMagic provides detailed reports for every text campaign and automation you create. Analyze your campaigns to figure out what is working and what isn’t.

Collect preferences from your customers to perform better targeting or get to know your audience better with SMS surveys. This will help you develop more relevant campaigns in the future.

What is the average cost of text messaging campaigns?

The cost of text messaging campaigns varies from one texting app to another. While other SMS providers offer monthly subscription plans, you pay only for what you use at TextMagic, so you have complete control over your budget.

Text campaign costs also depend on the country to which you are sending the texts. For example, the average price per 160-character text in the United States is $0.049, while in the United Kingdom it’s £0.04. This means that an SMS blast sent to 1,000 numbers in the U.S. will cost exactly $49.

SMS length can also impact the price of your communications. The number of SMS parts determines the final cost of your campaigns. The longer the text, the more you pay for your bulk campaigns. TextMagic calculates these costs as you compose your messages and lets you know exactly how much you will pay. You can also use our SMS length calculator to estimate the cost of your text messaging campaigns.

Please check out our SMS pricing page for details on all countries. We also offer a free calculator to help you estimate the average cost of sending mass text campaigns.

What is a toll-free number?

A toll-free number is a phone number that is free of charge for the calling party. Toll-free numbers are commonly used by businesses that want to encourage clients to reach out to them without charging extra fees for long-distance calls.

These numbers can also receive SMS as long as they are text-enabled. You can identify toll-free numbers by their three-digit prefixes similar to area codes (e.g., 833, 866, 888, 844, and 800 numbers). In North America, you must send your larger campaigns using a toll-free number or a 10DLC number.

What is the difference between bulk SMS, group SMS, SMS marketing, text marketing, mass text, and message blasts?

With so many business texting terms thrown around, it’s easy to become confused. We’ll explain the most common ones that you may encounter, as well as the differences between them:

  • Bulk SMS (or message blast): a texting method that allows you to send a large number of texts simultaneously, without having to send each message individually.
  • Group SMS: can be sent from a texting app to a specific group of contacts already on your list. All recipients of a group text will be added to one room. TextMagic does not offer group SMS capabilities. The main difference between bulk SMS and group SMS is that you can send completely different messages for bulk SMS, while group SMS are all the same.
  • Mass texting: is a marketing technique that enables businesses to send SMS, text notifications, and SMS appointment reminders to multiple subscribers instantly.
  • SMS marketing and text marketing refer to the same thing, but are used interchangeably. SMS (short message service) and text messaging describe the form of communication that allows users to send messages up to 160 characters to and from GSM devices. TextMagic allows you to send texts up to 918 characters long.
What is 10DLC, and how can it help your business?

10-digit long codes or A2P 10DLC numbers represent messaging solutions that allow businesses to send a large number of texts from a local number. In the past, 10-digit numbers were only used for P2P (person-to-person) messaging, but carriers have approved 10DLC for A2P (application-to-person) messaging.

10DLC numbers represent an ideal service for small businesses and enterprises because they have a higher throughput rate. Furthermore, these numbers are more effective than a vanity number or shortcode because they have a lower risk of spam. Lastly, 10-digit long codes are more cost-effective than shortcodes.

What is the difference between toll-free texting and 10DLC?

We are entering a new era of mobile texting as carriers are moving away from shared short codes. Starting March 2021, the main methods of sending bulk texts with TextMagic will be:

  • Via 10DLC (example: 409-432-2009)
  • Via toll-free numbers (example: 1-844-783-8888)

But which option is best for your business? Below are the main advantages and disadvantages of each business texting method:

  • 10DLC numbers
    • Advantages: can support high volumes of texts because they were explicitly designed for SMS marketing, more affordable than dedicated shortcodes, excellent deliverability, and better engagement rates.
    • Disadvantages: registering a 10DLC number can take up to three days, and 10DLC numbers have lower throughput rates as compared to dedicated shortcodes.
  • Toll-free numbers
    • Advantages: very easy to obtain, one-way and two-way SMS capabilities, high SMS throughput without extra fees, and secure.
    • Disadvantages: lower delivery rates because toll-free texts are subject to carrier filtering.
